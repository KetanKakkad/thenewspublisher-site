How It Works

Our powerful 3-step pipeline is the engine behind every publication.

1. Acquire

Pull structured news from trusted APIs, RSS feeds, your internal CMS, or our independent contributor hub, `Jrnalist.com`.

2. Enrich

Enrich content with AI — summaries, SEO metadata, and key points — tailored to your editorial voice using our `aiContext` engine.

3. Publish

Distribute content to Ghost, WordPress, or any connected CMS. Manage multiple brands and domains from one intelligent pipeline.
