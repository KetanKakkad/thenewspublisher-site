Our Secret Sauce: `aiContext` Models

Each TNP-powered publication is driven by its own `aiContext`—a set of detailed editorial principles that define what matters, who it matters to, and how it should be framed.

For `JudiciaryToday.com`

The `aiContext` filters for federal court rulings, Supreme Court decisions, and significant legal commentary, ignoring political fluff to deliver pure legal signal.

For `LayoffToday.com`

The `aiContext` identifies news on workforce reductions and corporate restructuring, then summarizes why it matters for the industry and the economy.

For `ICENewsToday.com`

The `aiContext` highlights news related to ICE, USCIS, and immigration. These stories are scattered all over the media landscape and often lost in the broader news cycle.

For `rx.news`

The `aiContext` prioritizes news on drug approvals, clinical trial results, and FDA regulatory shifts, providing targeted intelligence for the pharmaceutical industry.

For `africa.news`

The `aiContext` highlights stories of entrepreneurship and technology, filtering out stereotypical narratives to present a more authentic lens on the continent.

For `bondmarket.news`

The `aiContext` filters for analysis on yields, central bank policies, and sovereign debt, delivering noise-free insights for fixed-income professionals.
